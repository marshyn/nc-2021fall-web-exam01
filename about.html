About Fling Posse

Fling Posse is the Shibuya Division representative rap group. The team consists of the gambler Dice Arisugawa (right), a novelist Gentaro Yumeno (left), and their leader, fashion designer Ramuda Amemura (center).

Their many fans adore them due to the distinct characteristics of their performances, overflowing with a pop-style charm and provoking their opponents.


Hypnosis Mic -Division Rap Battle- (ヒプノシスマイク-Division Rap Battle-) is a Japanese multimedia project, created by King Records under their EVIL LINE RECORDS label. The project features eighteen voice actors and is centered on rap battles, with fans voting for their preferred team. The franchise has grown to include manga adaptations, live stage production, a mobile game, and an upcoming anime adaptation.
